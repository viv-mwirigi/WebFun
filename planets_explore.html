Mission Timeline

The journey to explore our solar system follows a carefully planned sequence of milestones:

Phase 1: Preparation (2025-2027)

Crew selection and training, spacecraft construction, and system testing

Phase 2: Earth Orbit Testing (2028)

Extended stay in Earth orbit to validate life support systems

Phase 3: Lunar Gateway (2029)

Establishment of lunar orbital station as a staging point

Phase 4: Mars Mission (2030-2033)

First crewed mission to the Martian system with surface exploration

Exploration Equipment

Our missions utilize cutting-edge technology designed for the harsh environments of space:

Orion Spacecraft

Next-generation crew vehicle for deep space exploration with advanced life support systems.

Mars Surface Habitat

Inflatable living modules with radiation shielding for extended stays on the Martian surface.

ROV-X Exploration Rover

Pressurized vehicle with 200km range for planetary surface exploration.

Advanced Spacesuits

Next-gen suits with enhanced mobility, radiation protection, and 8-hour life support.
