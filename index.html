Pong: The Game That Started It All

Released in 1972 by Atari, Pong was the first commercially successful video game. This simple tennis simulation featured two paddles and a square "ball."

Space Invaders: The Arcade Phenomenon

Taito's 1978 shooter created a cultural phenomenon. Players defended Earth from rows of descending aliens, with the iconic marching sound increasing tension.
